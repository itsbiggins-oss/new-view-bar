Welcome Home

For more than 40 years, New View Bar & Grill has been a Highview staple ‚Äî the kind of neighborhood spot that feels like home the second you walk in. We're not trying to be a fancy gastropub or some corporate chain from out of town. We're Louisville born and Louisville proud.


Locally owned and operated by Jamie, New View is built on the values this city knows best: treat people right, keep the drinks cold, and serve food that actually hits like a winner on Derby Day. Whether you're a regular who claims the same seat every visit or a first-timer looking for a laid-back game of pool, you're part of the family the moment you step inside.

Food That Hits The Spot

Our kitchen stays open late (serving until close to 3 AM!) because we know good times don't stop at dinner time. We're known for generous portions, fair prices, and food that's better than your average bar fare.
