Welcome Home

For over 40 years, New View Bar & Grill has been the heartbeat of Highview. We aren't trying to be a fancy gastropub or a corporate chain. We are a true neighborhood bar.


Locally owned and operated by Jamie Spaulding, New View is built on simple values: treat people right, pour a cold drink, and serve food that actually hits the spot. Whether you're a regular who has their own seat at the bar or a first-timer looking for a game of pool, you're welcome here.

Food That Hits The Spot

Our kitchen stays open late (serving until close to 4 AM!) because we know good times don't stop at dinner time. We're known for generous portions, fair prices, and food that's better than your average bar fare.
